PRIVACY POLICY

EFFECTIVE DATE: May 2025

At Meows & Brews, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when:

  • Signing up for newsletters or offers
  • Sending us a message via the contact form

This may include your name and email address. We also collect non- identifiable information through cookies and analytics tools such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages viewed on our site

This data helps us understand how users interact with our site and improve user experience.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website content and services through analytics

We do not use your information for profiling, spam, or unsolicited messages. You will only receive marketing emails if you explicitly opt in.

3. DATA PROTECTION AND SECURITY

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted forms, secured servers, and limited access to personal data. While no system is 100% secure, we are committed to doing our part in safeguarding your information.

4. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Payment processors (for secure transactions)
  • Email marketing tools
  • Hosting and analytics providers

These partners are required to keep your data confidential and secure.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we store about you
  • Ask for corrections or updates to that information
  • Request deletion of your data from our system

6. COOKIE POLICY

We use cookies to improve website functionality, remember user preferences, and collect analytics data. You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser at any time. By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies unless disabled.
